Our Specialized Services

Fast Services:

From cars to furniture, or agricultural products and care- packages to send home (wherever home may be!), Duo Logistics intends to maintain fast delivery. Wherever you are, from the point of pick-up we aim to have your shipment to its destination within 10 days. We have taken into consideration changes that may have occurred due to Brexit, and our freight forwarding process is currently not affected or implicated at all. We ensure that we are up to date with regulations and the law to keep up with our high-quality standards of delivery.

Offering FCL/LCL shipments:

With our varied services, you can have a full container shipment (FCL) that occupies the entire space of a container without having to share it with other merchandise. But if your goods only take up a small portion of a container, you may also choose an LCL shipment (shipped with other merchandise from other shippers in the same container). Duo Logistics will help you meet your needs by taking over the complexity of shipping for you.

Cargo services:

We ship commercial and personal/private cargoes. Whether you want to ship a collection of different goods in one container or a bulk of one product, Duo Logistics can create a strategic process to match your requirements.

How our Company works

Our independent logistics company have put together expertise from over 10 years of freight forwarding experiences to create and implement solutions to easy parcel deliveries from the EU to Africa. By providing the niche of air freight and sea freight services between just two continents, Duo Logistics are able to provide and offer the best prices for the fastest time of delivery. With our highly trained specialists, reliable links with storage centres, and advanced supply chain systems, we can focus on personal delivery services tailored to your shipment needs.
